The new Dezentrale Door-Locking-System

The Hackspace where I'm a member of will move soon.

But, because we are moving in a not so nice part of the city, we needed a new Door-Locking-System. A team, with a friend and me as the Team leaders where tasked to develop a new System for the new Hackspace Location.

I always has been interested in the mechanics of NFC and this Project tought me a lot. We needed to think of a Sysem that works reliably, fast and is secure. For this, we met with some other people of the Hackspace to discuss it.

The Result was, that we will use NFC-Cards and also make it possible to unluck the doors over an SSH connection. This would insure two ways if one would fail.

Unfortunatly, since of now, it has not been finally build. This is because we didn't moved yet. But there are some Proto types.

What is the "Dezentrale" You ask?

The "dezentrale e.V." is an independent non-profit association that runs a hackspace in the western part of Leipzig. A hackspace (also referred to here as "space") is a place for exchanging ideas and working on projects, which tend to be related to technology but don’t have to be. Occasionally, activities like sewing might take place as well. The space is a community-managed location where people with an interest in technology can come together to learn from each other, work on projects, or discuss (internet policy) topics. Perhaps you’ve heard of the Chaos Computer Club: We are the local Chaos in Leipzig. (We are an experience-sharing group of the Chaos Computer Club).
